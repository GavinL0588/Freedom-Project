Weak and Strong AI

Weak AI

Weak AI (Narrow AI) are Ais that are designed and trained to complete a specific task. Siri from Apple is an example of a weak AI.

Strong AI

Strong AI (Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)) is a programming that can replicate the cognitive abilities of the human brain. When they have a unfamiliar task they use, use fuzzy logic to apply knowledge from one domain to another and find a solution autonomously.

Not all AIs look like robots, some AIs can look like humans too, either in games or in real life
